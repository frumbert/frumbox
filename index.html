frumbox

an animating, iframe ony lightbox, written by tim st.clair - http://frumbert.org/ - licence: public domain

Will open over me.

Settable options

  • Position on screen and size
  • Overlay color, opacity and speed to display
  • Whether pressing escape key or clicking on the background closes
  • Whether close button appears
  • a future version will support opening from within an iframe (partially works now)
